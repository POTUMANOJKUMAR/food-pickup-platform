# Food Pickup Ordering Platform - Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Overview

A mobile application that allows users to pre-order food from nearby restaurants, pay online, and pick up their orders at a selected time without waiting in queues.

## Problem Statement

Customers currently face:

* Long waiting times at restaurants
* Additional delivery charges
* Delays caused by delivery partners
* Crowded pickup counters

## Proposed Solution

A food pre-order and self-pickup platform where customers can:

* Browse nearby restaurants
* View menus
* Place orders
* Schedule pickup times
* Pay online
* Receive pickup QR codes
* Collect food without waiting

## User Roles

### Customer

* Register/Login
* Browse restaurants
* Place orders
* Track orders
* Pickup orders

### Restaurant

* Manage menus
* Accept orders
* Update order status
* View reports

### Admin

* Manage restaurants
* Manage users
* Monitor orders
* Manage offers and banners

## MVP Scope

### Included

* Mobile Application
* Restaurant Panel
* Online Payments
* Order Tracking
* QR Pickup Verification
* Push Notifications

### Excluded

* Delivery Partners
* Loyalty Programs
* Referral Programs
* Multi-language Support
* Subscription Plans

## Success Metrics

* 500+ registered users
* 10+ restaurants onboarded
* 100+ orders per week
* Average rating above 4.0

## Revenue Model

### Restaurant Subscription

Monthly subscription fee.

### Commission Model

5%–10% commission per order.

### Featured Listings

Restaurants can pay for promotion.

## Launch Target

Phase 1:

* Chennai Pilot
* 5 to 10 Restaurants

Phase 2:

* City Expansion

Phase 3:

* Multi-City Rollout
